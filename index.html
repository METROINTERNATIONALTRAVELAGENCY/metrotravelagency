Day 1

Arrival & Dublin City Welcome

Arrival in Dublin in the evening, followed by check-in at Staycity Aparthotels Dublin City Centre in the Capel Street area. Guests are welcomed with complimentary tea or coffee before heading to The Brazen Head for dinner and a light music-forward visit to The Cobblestone.

Hotel: Staycity Aparthotels Dublin City Centre
Dinner: Irish stew or fish & chips + 1 drink
Evening mood: warm, historic, easy first-night pacing
Return to hotel: around 11:00 pm
Day 2

Dublin → Galway Trad Music Experience

Breakfast at the hotel, train to Galway, check-in at Snoozles Hostel Galway City Centre, lunch near Quay Street, a relaxed walking tour with Spanish Arch, then dinner and a full trad-focused night at Tigh Neachtain and The Crane Bar.

Breakfast included: eggs, sausage, toast, beans, coffee
Lunch included: seafood chowder or chicken pie + drink
Dinner included: roast meal or pasta + drink
Night highlights: Tigh Neachtain and The Crane Bar
Day 3

Connemara Experience + Final Trad Night

A scenic day of mountains, lakes, villages, and coastal views, followed by a farewell dinner and one more music-filled night back in Galway. This is the package’s balance point: nature by day, pub atmosphere by night.

Breakfast included before the guided tour
Lunch included: soup + sandwich + tea/coffee
Farewell dinner: Irish sharing platter + dessert
Return to hotel: around midnight
Day 4

Return & Departure

Breakfast, return to Dublin, quick photo and souvenir time in Temple Bar, lunch at Leo Burdock, then airport transfer based on your flight schedule. It closes the package with an iconic Dublin finish that still feels easy and manageable.
